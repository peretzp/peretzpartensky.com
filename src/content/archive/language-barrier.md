For the last couple of days, the author and their companion have been staying with a Spanish couple who speak very little English. Their communication relies on playing charades and occasionally consulting a dictionary. However, certain vocabulary doesn't require translation. The female host knows English numbers, while the male host knows the days of the week in English. Both take considerable pride in correcting each other within their respective areas of expertise.
