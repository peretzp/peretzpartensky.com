The author describes working in a newly constructed biotech building on a university campus near San Francisco's eastern dockyards. From higher floors, they can see downtown, the nearby highway, and Oakland's active shipping operations across the bay. The local dockyards are largely vacant but attract urban explorers.

During an expedition with friends to an abandoned warehouse across from Burning Man headquarters, the group discovered two other people fishing inside. One individual had a distinctive tattoo on his neck reading "FUCK THE POLICE" in two-inch lettering. When the author remarked that "that's not something you whisper," the person laughed and showed them his catch of yellow perch.

The author also noticed stickers among the fisherman's belongings that had "sprouted all over town" and attempted to locate an image online labeled "as seen on cl" to share with readers. The piece captures an unexpected encounter that blends urban exploration with an ironic moment of levity.
