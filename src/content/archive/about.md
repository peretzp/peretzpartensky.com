For the summer of 2008, the author held a visiting fellowship at Peking University in Beijing, China, sharing experiences in real time on the blog. The site's mission focuses on original content presented as discrete vignettes, communicating discoveries with the immediacy of first-hand revelation.

The author emphasizes science as a primary focus, seeking to share fascination through explanatory entries concentrating on molecular biology and its applications to social understanding. Additional interests include metrics related to microcredit initiatives.
