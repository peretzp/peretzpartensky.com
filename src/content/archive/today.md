The author describes being awakened by someone persistently asking for "mike" through the bedroom window. The visitor stuck his head through the window during breakfast, scratching at the screen repeatedly. Eventually realizing the person was harmless, the author allowed him to continue his search. The man eventually gave up, cursing "mike" for giving him the wrong address, blaming himself for not remembering it correctly, and expressing frustration that "the world" wasn't "upside down" before disappearing.
