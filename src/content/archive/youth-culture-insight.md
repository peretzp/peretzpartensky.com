An articulate young girl delivered closing remarks during an impromptu presentation by her juvenile group. Though her exact words remained unclear, the entire ensemble rapidly departed with considerable commotion when the author mentioned his intention to "hand these videos over to the authorities."

A video titled "Young Girls Smoking in Christ's Pieces, Cambridge, UK" was captured during a park stroll in Cambridge with a companion named Dana in 2004.

The post includes dialogue snippets from the encounter:

"Cause I want to smoke"

Dana – "Is smoking Cool?"

"Yeah! Yeah!"

"I'm high, so high."

"Do these look like chocolate cigarettes?"

The author notes that the children featured are now adults, making the video's publication appropriate.
