While taking a walk with his mobile's earpiece, Rupert swatted an innocent male horsefly (though he later mistakenly identified it as a wasp) which was, at that moment of contact with the palm of his hand immediately preceding the swat, in precisely the same mental state as the person of whom Rupert was talking was presently experiencing–abject boredom in the most significant moments of life. For you see, Malbert was dying and the whole town knew about it.
