The author describes spending an hour handwriting thoughts with a mechanical pencil, becoming enamored with both their handwriting and the writing instrument itself. They express newfound appreciation for mechanical pencils during this period, noting the pencil served as their primary form of social output. The author decides they want to purchase a higher-quality mechanical pencil capable of maintaining a sharp tip through extended writing arcs while minimizing lead breakage and rotation issues.
