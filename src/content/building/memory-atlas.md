MemoryAtlas is a Python CLI that turns Apple Voice Memos into structured, searchable Obsidian notes. It processes audio through transcription, enrichment (summaries, topics, sentiment), and publication into a vault.

929 memos. 675 hours of voice. All indexed, all searchable.
